About the club

Career readiness meets entrepreneurial energy.

InnoPitch & Greenovation Mavericks Club is a student-focused initiative led by Hemapriyan.T and supported by a team of passionate learners. The club nurtures professional growth through career workshops, mentoring, networking, startup awareness, and hands-on pitch experience.

Our vision is to cultivate a generation of students who are innovative, confident, and ready to create opportunities for themselves and others. We help members sharpen their communication, leadership, problem-solving, and pitching skills while exploring both career pathways and entrepreneurship.

What students gain

Resume building, interview preparation, soft-skill development, startup exposure, networking with mentors and professionals, and clearer career direction.

What we offer

Career workshops, entrepreneurship bootcamps, guest lectures, networking events, pitch competitions, hackathons, idea incubator sessions, and internship guidance.

What we do

Activities that build confidence and opportunity

From career workshops to startup pitch challenges, every activity is designed to help students grow into stronger professionals and innovators.

Career workshops

Resume building, LinkedIn optimization, interview skills, and personal branding sessions that prepare students for internships and jobs.

Entrepreneurship sessions

Bootcamps on ideation, pitching, startup basics, idea incubation, and funding awareness for future founders.

Networking & mentoring

Guest lectures, alumni talks, industry conversations, and mentoring opportunities that connect students with professionals.

Innovation challenges

Pitch-it-fast sessions, hackathons, story-pitching activities, and startup challenges that turn ideas into action.
